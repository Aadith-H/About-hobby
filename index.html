My favorite hobby is Engineering

Hi!

My favorite Hobby is Building stuff. I like it because it is fun and it teaches me about engineering skills. I love building things!
From making paper airplanes that can actually fly to constructing miniature bridges with straws and glue, engineering lets me turn everyday stuff into awesome inventions.
There are so many different types of engineering. There's the kind that builds skyscrapers and bridges, making cities look like futuristic playgrounds.
Then there's the one that sends rockets to space, exploring the great unknown. I even heard about bioengineers who work with living things to make the world a better place. It's like being a part of a club where each member has a unique skill set.
I also like coding. It ties to engineering and I can program things that I make. Engineering and coding are like having a superpower. You get to create things that others might think are impossible.
The coolest part is that coding is like a secret language that lets people like me almost talk to computers. It's not as hard as it sounds! I started with simple stuff, like making my computer say "Hello, World!" but now I can make games, websites, and even control gadgets with just a few clicks and clacks on the keyboard.
